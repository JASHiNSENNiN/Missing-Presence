Instead...
He continues working.
Tightening bolts.
Organizing tools.
Finding one more thing to fix.
Anything...
Except what is waiting for him at home.
His phone vibrates.
A message from JENNIFER.
"Pauwi ka na?"
RICARDO glances at it.
Then slips the phone back into his pocket.
Without replying.
He returns to work.

INT. ILAGAN HOUSE – LIVING ROOM – SAME TIME
JENNIFER sits alone on the couch.
The television plays quietly in the background.
She isn't watching.
Her attention never leaves her phone.
She scrolls through social media.
Perfect families.
Successful children.
Graduation photos.
She quietly presses the "Like" button.
Again.
And again.
And again.
She pauses at the photo she posted earlier of MAYA.
Hundreds of reactions.
Comments praising her daughter.
JENNIFER forces a small smile.
Then continues scrolling.

INT. ILAGAN HOUSE – HALLWAY – LATER
RICARDO quietly enters the house.
JENNIFER briefly looks up.
JENNIFER
Kumain ka na?
RICARDO
Oo.
(JENNIFER nods.)
She returns to her phone.
RICARDO quietly walks past her.
Toward their bedroom.
Neither notices...
That MAYA's bedroom light is still on upstairs.
Neither asks if she's awake.
Or how she's doing.
The house falls silent once more.

INT. MAYA'S BEDROOM – CONTINUOUS
MAYA finally stops scrolling.
She locks her phone.
The room goes dark.
For a brief moment...
She catches her own reflection on the black screen.
She stares at it.
The reflection feels unfamiliar.
As if she's looking at someone else.
Someone who stopped dreaming a long time ago.
She quietly places the phone beside her pillow.
Lies down.
And closes her eyes.

INT. ILAGAN HOUSE – WIDE SHOT – NIGHT
The house stands still.
Upstairs...
A daughter lies awake in silence.
Downstairs...
A father buries himself in work.
A mother loses herself in endless scrolling.
Three people.
Three separate rooms.
Three separate lives.
Living beneath the same roof.
Yet farther apart than they have ever been.
It's measured in the conversations they never had.
The truths they never listened to.
And the love they forgot to show.
The Ilagan family never stopped loving one another.
But somewhere between expectations...
And the glow of their screens...
They stopped being present.
The house remains quiet.
The phones continue glowing.
No one looks up.
FADE TO BLACK.
THE END.
